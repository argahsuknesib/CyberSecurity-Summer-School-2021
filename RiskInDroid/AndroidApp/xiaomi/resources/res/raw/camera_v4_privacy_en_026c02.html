TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

We will require you to provide personal information that is necessary for us to successfully deliver our services. Without such personal information, we may not be able to provide our products or services to you.

We may collect the following types of information (which may or may not be personal information):

  • Account information: We will collect information related to your Mi Account and the accounts for device sharing.
  • Membership information: We will collect information about your purchased membership, including membership type, order ID, payment time and amount, as well as remaining days for valid membership.
  • Device related information: We will collect information about the hardware, performance and status of your device, including device ID (DID), firmware version, device location (such as in the living room), time zone, and security code (if any) for accessing the device, the pairing code required to bind the device, as well as memory card information (storage status, remaining storage, storage on/off, recording mode) and screen information (such as definition, scale ratio you set).
  • Information about device settings: We may collect information about device settings, including status light on/off status, data usage prevention on/off status, sleep settings, image settings, night vision settings, motion tracking on/off, Home Surveillance Assistant settings (sensitivity, home surveillance period, alert time interval, motion tracking on/off, alert message push on/off, push type), video playing in floating window, cloud calibration, and AI settings (face recognition on/off, baby crying sound detection on/off).
  • Network information: We may collect your network information when your device is connecting to a network, including the current Wi-Fi connecting mode (such as LAN mode, remote mode), assigned IP address, Wi-Fi signal strength, RSSI, MAC address, as well as name (SSID) and password of the Wi-Fi network provided by you. Such information will be encrypted and stored in a secure server and will be used for the purposes that you have authorized.
  • Information about live image: We may collect information about live image, including current image time, playback speed, image quality option, image size and sound volume. In addition, you may snap a screenshot or record the live image and save on your phone.
  • Voice recording information: We may collect voice recording information, including your recorded voice, recording time and duration.
  • Video recording information: The device will record video when triggering events occur, including when image of detection area changes, moving people are detected, baby crying sound is detected, visitor is a noted face or not in the system, smart scenario is triggered. To do this, we will collect information about video recording, including recording date and time, triggering events, recording time length, sound settings, and play speed. If a memory card is used, you can choose to save the recorded videos in the recording mode you set on the memory card.
  • Face recognition information: When you enable face recognition and authorize us to collect information after you fully understand the feature, we will collect facial photos provided by you or recognized by the system from videos, facial features recognized from photos, facial notes you input and recognition results. If you do not provide us with such information, you will not be able to use the facial recognition function normally, but it will not affect your use of other functions. For such information, we will take strict security precautions, including irreversible transformation processing and encrypted storage to ensure the security of such data. Here are more details on how we use your information (which may include personal information)

  • Pairing with device and synchronizing data: We will collect information about accounts, device and network to synchronize data, enable the device to accept plug-in instruction, and upload data to server (applicable to specific functions/services only).
  • Viewing live image: You can view the live image from time to time on your phone. For this reason, we will collect information about current image time, playback speed, image quality option, image size and sound volume. In addition, you can snap a screenshot or record the current live image. Live image screenshot/video recording will be saved on your phone instead of being uploaded to any server. You can also control camera angle with direction button. To do this, we will forward your control commands to the device.
  • Home Surveillance Assistant: You can choose to enable or disable this feature. If enabled, video recording will be triggered when image within the detection range of the camera changes. You can set the starting and end time of home surveillance, as well as the shooting interval which can avoid interference from one same event. You can view the videos online on your phone. To do this, we will collect information about your settings on sensitivity, home surveillance period, alert sensitivity, alert time interval, alert message push notification on/off, push type, etc.
  • Video playback: You can view various videos recorded in the plug-in. To do this, we will collect both triggering events and videos recorded, and encrypt and save them in a secure server. You can download, view or delete them during specified period, or you can upgrade your membership for an extended storage period to view videos recorded in earlier days. To do this, we will collect information about network, your membership, and video recording, including recording date and time, triggering events, recording time length, sound settings, and play speed.
  • Baby crying sound detection: When baby crying sound detection is enabled, you will receive push notifications when baby crying sound is detected. We will process such information locally and will not upload it to the cloud. To do this, we will collect information about your membership and baby crying sound push notification on/off.
  • Facial recognition: You can enable or disable the facial recognition function. If enabled, you can add a face (by providing a facial photo or adding one recognized directly from a video), add notes to a face and recognize a face. As a result, when a face is detected, the device can extract its facial characteristic values and compare the values with those of previously recognized faces, so as to identify whether the face is noted or not. To do this, we will collect information about facial photos you provide, screenshot of faces from videos, facial characteristic values extracted from photos and facial recognition result (whether the face is noted or not). You can rest assured that such information is stored in a secure server and will only be used for the purposes you have authorized. We will ask for your permission again if we want to use such information for other purposes in the future. If you no longer want to use this function, please go to Settings to disable it.
  • Managing memory card: You can choose to save the videos on a memory card, so you can view them at any time. When the storage space on the memory card is low, the older videos will be overwritten by newer ones. You can manage your videos in the plug-in as necessary. To do this, we will collect your memory card information (storage status, remaining storage, storage on/off, recording mode).
  • Two-way talkback: You can view information about the live image in the plug-in and talk with the visitor with two-way talkback function. We will not collect information about your conversation, unless you select to record current live image (saved locally) before/when using the two-way talkback. But under no circumstances will we try to read or identify your conversation.
  • Collecting user feedback: The feedback you provide is extremely valuable in helping us improve our services. In order to follow up on the feedback you have chosen to provide, Imilab may correspond with you using the personal information that you have provided and keep records.
  • Sending notifications: The device will send a notification if a triggering event occurs. For example, it will send a push notification to your phone when image changes. To do this, we will collect information about your push settings. You can disable the push notification function in "Settings > Home Surveillance Assistant > Push type setting".
