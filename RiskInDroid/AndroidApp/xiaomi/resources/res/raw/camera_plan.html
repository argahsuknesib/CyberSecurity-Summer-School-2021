Collection of information

Users partake in the program only disclose the following data to ZMI:

1.Clicking status of your camera’s settings, such as clicking of the auto-rotate and sleeping modes, in order for us to make accurate judgment and adjustment to the parameter settings of the camera.

2.Product function usage status, such as the settings of your Security Helper, as well as the duration and frequency of live videos you watch. We do so in order to collect specific usage status of all functions, to make better judgment on the importance of every function and adjust the priority of R & D of new functions.

We respect your privacy, and please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information: [http://service.zmifi.com/doc/privacy/ipcamera/gdpr_en.html]/

Use of Information

ZMI hereby promises that ZMI will abide by all relevant national laws and regulations. The information above will be held under strict confidentiality, and it will only be used to improve our products and services. It will not be offered to any third party that is not relevant to the above purpose.

Joining and withdrawal of program

If you wish, you can voluntarily enable or disable the program in “Settings-General Settings-Privacy Settings-User Experience Program” of the homepage of “Mi Home Security Camera Basic 1080P”. Enabling the program means you choose to join this User Experience Improvement Program; you can withdraw from the program by disabling it. Please note: for your camera's reliance on the network connection, turning off the program when your device is offline might not be effective until the device is reconnected to the network.
