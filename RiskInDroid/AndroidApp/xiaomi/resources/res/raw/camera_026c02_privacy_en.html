In order to provide our services to you, we will ask you to provide personal information that is necessary to provide those services to you. If you do not provide your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our products or services.

  • Video recording information: When home surveillance assistant is enabled, video recording function will be triggered when someone moves within the surveillance area, babe crying sound is detected, and/or other artificial intelligence scenes. Therefore, we may collect information related to such recording function, including recording date and duration, triggering event, play speed and sound setting. If you use a storage card, you can also choose to store the video on the storage card in the recording mode you set. We will also collect information about events that trigger video recording, including area change, someone passing by, baby crying sound, and smart scenarios trigger.
