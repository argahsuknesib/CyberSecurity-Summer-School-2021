Joining and withdrawal of program

The user may opt in/out the User Experience Improvement Plan at their own discretion by going to the Home screen of Mi 360° Home Security Camera 1080p Essential and, on the upper right corner, tapping "Settings > General settings > Legal info > User Experience Improvement Plan". By enabling it, the user chooses to opt in the User Experience Improvement Plan; by disabling it, the User chooses to opt out the User Experience Improvement Plan.

 

Please note: To apply camera settings requires Internet connection; therefore, if you disable the feature when the device is offline, the settings will only be applied when the device is connected to the Internet again.
